## Review-stage and legal routes

These URLs are intentionally `noindex,follow` and excluded from the sitemap:

- English editorial hub and five drafts: `/resources`, `/resources/how-to-source-products-from-yiwu`, `/resources/1688-vs-alibaba-for-ecommerce-sellers`, `/resources/china-fulfillment-cost-guide`, `/resources/china-vs-local-warehouse`, `/resources/how-to-calculate-landed-cost`.
- Spanish editorial hub and all 30 routes under `/es/recursos/*`.
- Legal operational summaries: `/privacy`, `/terms`, `/cookie-policy`.

Before an article becomes indexable, add article-specific body copy, a named author or reviewer, real publication and modified dates, useful internal links, and primary-source citations where external facts are discussed. Then add Article schema and restore the route to sitemap. Legal pages should remain noindex unless qualified legal review approves them for indexation.

## GEO publication standard

Every future indexable page should provide, in this order:

1. A direct definition or answer in the first substantive paragraph.
2. The audience and use case.
3. Explicit inputs, process, responsibilities, boundaries, and next step.
4. Short sections that can be quoted independently.
5. Visible FAQs only when they answer real page-specific questions.
6. Named author/reviewer and real dates for editorial content.
7. Primary sources for external laws, platform requirements, market data, or statistics.
8. Matching canonical, social metadata, structured data, and internal links.
