# YIWULANE Brand Notes

## Meaning

YIWULANE suggests a practical route from Yiwu to the customer.

## Brand expression

**YIWULANE**  
Sourcing · QC · Fulfillment

## Taglines

Primary:

> From Yiwu to Your Customers.

Alternative:

> Test from China. Scale with Local Inventory.

## Personality

- experienced;
- accountable;
- direct;
- calm;
- evidence-led;
- modern without pretending to be a software platform.

## Words to use

- sourcing plan;
- incoming QC;
- agreed specification;
- low-MOQ test;
- branded packaging;
- fulfillment workflow;
- transparent cost structure;
- local inventory;
- practical route;
- accountable team.

## Words to avoid

- winning products;
- guaranteed profit;
- cheapest;
- fastest;
- millions of products;
- fully automated;
- zero risk;
- hassle-free;
- best supplier;
- worldwide in X days.
