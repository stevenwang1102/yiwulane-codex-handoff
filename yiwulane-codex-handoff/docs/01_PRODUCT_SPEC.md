# Product Specification

## 1. Brand

**Name:** YIWULANE  
**Descriptor:** Sourcing · QC · Fulfillment  
**Primary tagline:** From Yiwu to Your Customers.  
**Primary positioning:** Your Yiwu sourcing and fulfillment team.

YIWULANE helps growing overseas e-commerce sellers source products, control quality, create branded packaging, and fulfill orders through China and local inventory.

## 2. Site type

A premium English-language B2B lead-generation website.

It is not:

- an online product marketplace;
- a low-price dropshipping directory;
- a self-service SaaS dashboard;
- an educational “how to get rich” site.

## 3. Ideal customers

### Primary

- Shopify and DTC sellers;
- TikTok Shop sellers;
- Amazon FBA sellers;
- growing e-commerce brands without a full China sourcing team.

### Best-fit profile

- already has a store or specific products;
- approximately 10–200 orders per day, while not excluding smaller qualified pilots;
- 5–100 active SKUs;
- values quality, transparency, packaging, and accountability;
- wants to test with low inventory before scaling locally.

### Poor-fit leads

- no product, no store, and no budget;
- asks only for “winning products”;
- expects free sourcing work indefinitely;
- requires counterfeit, infringing, prohibited, or high-risk products.

## 4. Core service model

1. **YIWULANE SOURCE** — product sourcing and supplier management.
2. **YIWULANE CHECK** — incoming QC and evidence.
3. **YIWULANE BRAND** — labels, inserts, bundling, and packaging.
4. **YIWULANE FULFILL** — China warehousing and direct fulfillment.
5. **YIWULANE SCALE** — bulk replenishment and local inventory.

## 5. Primary offer

### Start a 3-SKU Pilot

A structured first engagement for up to three products. It may include:

- product and supplier research;
- MOQ and lead-time comparison;
- sample coordination;
- basic quality observations;
- packaging options;
- fulfillment route recommendation;
- estimated landed-cost structure;
- one review call.

Pricing is not yet approved. The site must not display a price. Use “Request pilot details” or “Pilot fee is quoted based on product complexity.” A future paid pilot may be partially credited toward a production order, but do not promise this until approved.

## 8. Conversion paths

### Primary

Landing page → relevant service/solution → trust evidence → `/pilot` → qualified lead.

### Secondary

Landing page → `/how-it-works` → `/quote` or booking link.

### Tertiary

Resource article → contextual CTA → service/solution → pilot.

## 9. Required trust signals

Use only truthful signals available now:

- based in Yiwu, China;
- 20 years of international trade experience;
- low-MOQ sourcing approach;
- QC before fulfillment;
- China + local inventory planning;
- transparent cost breakdown philosophy;
- one accountable operating team.

Do not present unverified office, warehouse, staff, customer, order, partner, certification, or country-count claims.

## 10. Platform positioning

### Shopify and DTC

Source, brand, test from China, and move winners to local inventory.

### TikTok Shop

Sample coordination, small-batch preparation, demand-spike planning, and local-stock scaling. Do not promise that China-direct shipping meets every local platform requirement.

### Amazon FBA

Sourcing, product preparation, labeling, bundling, inspection, carton preparation, and bulk shipment planning. Do not position seller-to-consumer China dropshipping as the main Amazon solution.
