# SEO and Analytics

## Search intent

Prioritize visitors with an active sourcing or fulfillment need.

Core themes:

- Yiwu sourcing agent;
- China sourcing for Shopify;
- China fulfillment for e-commerce;
- branded dropshipping fulfillment;
- low-MOQ private label sourcing;
- TikTok Shop supply-chain support;
- Amazon FBA sourcing and preparation;
- e-commerce quality control in China;
- China and local warehouse hybrid fulfillment.

Do not promise rankings or create thin keyword doorway pages.

## Structured data

Allowed claims:

- brand name;
- website;
- Yiwu, China as area served/from-location only when business details are approved;
- business email/phone after approval;
- service descriptions;
- founder experience claim.

Do not add:

- aggregate ratings;
- fake reviews;
- price ranges not approved;
- certifications not held;
- geographic offices not operated.

## Analytics events

Implement only after consent:

- `primary_cta_click`
- `secondary_cta_click`
- `pilot_form_start`
- `pilot_form_step_complete`
- `pilot_form_submit_success`
- `pilot_form_submit_error`
- `booking_click`
- `whatsapp_click`
- `service_page_view`
- `solution_page_view`

Never include product details, email, phone, store URL, or other PII in analytics event parameters.

## Search Console and launch

After final domain confirmation:

- verify the domain property;
- submit sitemap;
- inspect core routes;
- monitor indexing and canonical errors;
- monitor branded and commercial-intent queries;
- do not promote draft resource articles until reviewed.
